Top Performers

Justin Tucker has consistently came through in tough situations for the Ravens. This season, he has improved his Hall Of Fame caliber resume by including the longest kick in NFL history and more clutch kicks.

Mark Andrews has already eclipsed 1000 yards with 3 games remaining. He has been a steady reciever and a capable blocker all season.

Continuing the Ravens legacy of great special teams performers, Duvernay has been named to his first pro bowl this season. He is a swiss-army knife when it comes to athleticism.

Brown has lived up to his first-round status with a very high quality season. His speed is evident and his willingness to fight for yards is obvious.

“Greatness is a lot of small things done well. Day after day, workout after workout, obedience after obedience, day after day.” Ray Lewis is arguably the best MLB of all time. He was a monster and a leader on the field. He will forever be one of the greatest Ravens to ever play.

-Ray Lewis
